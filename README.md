## 🚀 **Revolutionary Features**

### 🎙️ **Voice Set Tracking: Hands-Free Logging**
Speak your sets during workouts and watch them automatically appear in your log. No more fumbling with your phone between sets.

**What You Can Say:**
- *"I did bench press 70kg 10 reps"*
- *"Just finished curls 20kg 12 reps"*
- *"Completed shoulder press 30kg 8 reps"*

**The Magic:**
- Advanced AI understands natural speech patterns
- Automatically corrects common speech recognition errors
- Context-aware matching to your workout routine
- Works seamlessly on iOS Safari and all major browsers

### 📸 **AI Food Vision: Instant Nutrition Analysis**
Point, shoot, and get complete nutritional breakdown instantly. Our AI analyzes your food photos to provide accurate macro calculations.

**How It Works:**
- Take a photo of your meal
- Add optional notes (like "no oil" or "extra cheese")
- Get instant calories, protein, carbs, and fat breakdown
- Automatic daily macro tracking

### 🏛️ **War Journal Dashboard: Your Command Center**

- **Discipline Streak**: See your consistency in action

- **Smart Workout Rotation**: Never wonder what to train today
- **Performance Insights**: Track your strength gains over time
